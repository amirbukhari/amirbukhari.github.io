About

Amir Bukhari is a software engineer based in Oakville, Ontario. He's currently a Lead Software Engineer at Rentsync, where he architects and ships software powering Canada's rental industry.

Amir has a strong passion for building clean, functional software and sharing his creations with the world. His background spans full-stack web development, cloud infrastructure, research tooling, and game dev.

He graduated from Niagara College's Computer Programmer Analyst program and has since worked across startups, university research labs, and enterprise environments.
